Our Approach

Expert Guidance
for New Businesses

Choosing the right business structure is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a business owner. The wrong entity type can cost you thousands in unnecessary taxes and expose you to personal liability you didn't expect.

We sit down with you, understand your goals and situation, and recommend the right structure for your specific needs — whether that's a sole proprietorship, LLC, S-Corp, or C-Corp. Then we handle the registration paperwork with the Minnesota Secretary of State.

We also handle the EIN application, initial business tax setup, and connect you with the right resources to keep your new business compliant from day one.

Business Structures We Handle

Sole Proprietorship
Simplest structure, no formal registration required. Best for solo, low-risk businesses.
LLC
Most popular choice. Limited liability protection with flexible tax treatment options.
S-Corporation
Avoids double taxation. Ideal for established businesses with consistent profits.
Partnership
For two or more owners. We set up the partnership agreement and tax structure correctly.
