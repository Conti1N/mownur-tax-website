Refund Timelines

How Long Does It Take?

Federal · E-Filed
E-File + Direct Deposit
21 days
Most refunds arrive within 21 days of the IRS accepting your return. Direct deposit is fastest.
Federal · Paper Check
E-File + Paper Check
4–6 weeks
Paper checks take significantly longer to arrive than direct deposit. We always recommend direct deposit.
Minnesota State
MN Direct Deposit
2–3 weeks
Minnesota state refunds typically arrive within 2–3 weeks of the state accepting your return via e-file.
