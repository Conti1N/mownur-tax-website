Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Mownur Tax Services LLC · Rochester, MN

Overview

Mownur Tax Services LLC ("Mownur Tax Services," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our website at mownurservices.com or engage our tax preparation and related services.

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Identification information: First name, last name, date of birth
  • Tax identification: Last four digits of your Social Security Number (we never store full SSNs)
  • Contact information: Email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Financial information: Income documents (W-2s, 1099s), bank account information for direct deposit of refunds, business income and expense records
  • Documents: Files you upload through our document portal
  • Appointment information: Date, time, and type of service requested

We also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our website, such as your browser type, referring URL, and general location (city/region level only).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Prepare and file your federal and state tax returns
  • Communicate with you about your return status, appointments, and account
  • Send you important tax deadline reminders and updates (you can opt out at any time)
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with applicable federal and state tax laws and regulations

How We Protect Your Information

The security of your financial and personal data is our highest priority. We employ industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Encryption of all data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure document storage using Microsoft OneDrive with access controls
  • Staff access limited strictly to what is required to perform their job functions
  • We never store full Social Security Numbers — only the last four digits for client identification
  • We never share your tax documents or return information with third parties for marketing purposes

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • IRS and state tax authorities: When required to file your tax returns on your behalf with your consent
  • Service providers: Third-party vendors that help us operate our business (document storage, appointment scheduling) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • With your consent: Any other sharing requires your explicit consent

Data Retention

We retain your tax records for a minimum of seven (7) years in accordance with IRS recordkeeping requirements. After this period, documents are securely destroyed. You may request a copy of your records or deletion of records not required to be retained by law at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of information we are not legally required to retain
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (507) 319-7168 or visit us at 1207 7th St NW #3, Rochester, MN 55901.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under age 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child's information has been provided to us without proper consent, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
