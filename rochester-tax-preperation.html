What We Do

Personal &
Business Returns

Mownur Tax Services prepares both federal and Minnesota state tax returns for individuals, families, and small businesses. Whether you're filing a simple W-2 return or navigating self-employment income, we apply every credit and deduction you're entitled to.

We specialize in serving clients with unique situations: new immigrants, mixed-status families, self-employed individuals, and small business owners. No situation is too complex.

When you file with us, we don't just enter numbers — we review your entire situation to make sure nothing is missed. Every return we submit is error-free and IRS-compliant.

What's Included

  • Federal & State ReturnsIRS Form 1040 + Minnesota M1 for residents and non-residents.
  • All Income TypesW-2, 1099, self-employment, rental, investments, foreign income.
  • Credits & DeductionsChild Tax Credit, EITC, education credits, business deductions and more.
  • Bilingual ServiceAvailable in English and Somali — no language barrier.
