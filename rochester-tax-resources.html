Know Your Rights

Taxpayer Bill of Rights

  • 01The Right to Be Informed — You have the right to know what you need to do to comply with the tax laws.
  • 02The Right to Quality Service — You have the right to receive prompt, courteous, and professional assistance from the IRS.
  • 03The Right to Pay No More Than the Correct Amount — You have the right to pay only the amount of tax legally due.
  • 04The Right to Challenge the IRS's Position — You have the right to raise objections and provide documentation in response to IRS actions.
  • 05The Right to Appeal an IRS Decision — You have the right to a fair and impartial administrative appeal of most IRS decisions.
  • 06The Right to Finality — You have the right to know the maximum amount of time you have to challenge the IRS's position.
  • 07The Right to Privacy — You have the right to expect that any IRS inquiry or enforcement action will comply with the law.
  • 08The Right to Confidentiality — You have the right to expect that your tax information will not be disclosed except as authorized by law.
  • 09The Right to Retain Representation — You have the right to retain an authorized representative of your choice to represent you in dealings with the IRS.
  • 10The Right to a Fair and Just Tax System — You have the right to expect the tax system to consider your ability to pay.
